# Public bundle notice

This archive is distributed as a research record for analysis reproduction.
It intentionally omits `data/sources/`, score/source PDFs and images, and all
scratch directories. Inclusion in the archive does not imply that omitted
source scans may be redistributed.

Four Mozart dossier files and their derived tabulations use data from the
DCMLab Mozart Piano Sonatas corpus, release v2.3, commit
`5337257a5318711e6302cfe85c3f1a6ade3c6271`. The corpus authors are Jann-Ruben
Hentschel, Markus Neuwirth, and Martin Rohrmeier; the associated article is
doi:10.5334/tismir.63. DCML corpus data are licensed under
[CC BY-NC-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/).
The corpus's NMA score PDF is not included.

The C. P. E. Bach and Benda dossier files are investigator transcriptions from
public-domain first-edition authorities. The bundle provides provenance and
hashes, not the scanned editions. Legibility editions and the Clementi/Brownell
replication sources are also excluded, regardless of their individual rights
status.

Raw model responses are preserved as generated research records. Provider and
model names identify tested systems; they do not imply provider endorsement.
No blanket relicensing of third-party material is asserted. Consult
`data/provenance/source-manifest.json` for file-level source, citation, and
rights notes.
